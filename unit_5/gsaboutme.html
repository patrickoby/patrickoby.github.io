Throwing Shade Since 2012

Grunge Shades is a little business borne from the blog Grunge Children and evolved out of a little twelvie's love of unique shades.

The Grunge Children brand and blog was founded in 2012 by Laneikka. The blog came to be in a little Sydney basement and provides an edgy outlook of weekly ootd's and lifestyle advice on how to be the baddest (and most confident!) gal around.

Laneikka's love for styling developed at only twelve years old and is still going strong. Her passion for unique sunglasses is what led her to curating her own line of "Grunge Shades". Her main aim is to keep you looking shady as F!
